## Battery and range
The Lumina X1 ships with a 720 Wh removable battery. Under Eco mode it has a
tested range of 95 kilometers on a single charge. In Turbo mode, expect about
48 kilometers. A full charge from empty takes 4.5 hours with the standard
charger, or 2.5 hours with the optional FastCharge dock.

## Motor and speed
It uses a rear-hub 500 W motor with a peak output of 750 W. The assisted top
speed is limited to 32 km/h to comply with Class 3 e-bike regulations.

## Price and warranty
The Lumina X1 retails for $2,480. It comes with a 3-year warranty on the frame
and a 2-year warranty on the battery and electronics. Registered owners get an
extra 12 months of battery coverage for free.

## Maintenance
Lumina recommends a tune-up every 1,000 km. The chain should be lubricated every
250 km. Firmware updates are delivered through the Lumina Ride app over Bluetooth;
the current firmware version is 3.4.1.

## Support
Customer support is reachable at support@luminacycles.example from 8am to 6pm
Pacific. The warranty claim turnaround is typically 7 business days.
